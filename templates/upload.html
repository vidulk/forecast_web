Anonymising Your Data

Anonymising your data is essential to maintain privacy while using public tools. Here are a few simple steps:

  • Remove any columns containing personally identifiable information (names, addresses, etc.).
  • Replace unique identifiers (e.g., customer IDs) with random numbers.
  • Scale sensitive numeric data (e.g., sales figures) to a standard range, such as 0-1, using a technique like Min-Max scaling.
  • Test the anonymisation by ensuring the original data cannot be reconstructed.
